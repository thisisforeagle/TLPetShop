About Pet Shop

Learn about the origin of Pet Shop and the people working to take your pet experience to the next level.

Our Mission

To build and create a self-sustaining ecosystem that enables and facillitates pet adoption around the country.

Our Vision

To become the biggest pet loving community in Ireland.

What is Pet Shop?

Pet Shop is a family-owned pet shop that sells a variety of animals and offers pet services. We strive to find the perfect pet for every home and the perfect home for every pet. For this, we respect everyone equally, from customers to pets.

Here, in our website, you can adopt many different types of pets and buy the best pet food, toys and items for them.

Our History

On April 17th 1868, the eventual owners of Pet Shop found seven stray puppies and kittens on the street. Not being able to handle all of them, they looked for friends to take care of them, but vowed to still help as much as they could, so a few weeks later, they opened the first location as a pet care centre.

As word got out of this new establishment, demand for their services skyrocketed, forcing Pet Shop to evolve and provide a variety of new services. Nowadays, Pet Shop is the biggets pet company in Limerick, and they count with an inmense catalog of food, toys, and even pets!
